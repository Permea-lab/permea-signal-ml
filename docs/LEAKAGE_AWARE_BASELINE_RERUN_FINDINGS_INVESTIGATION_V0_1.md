## Interpretation

The safest interpretation is: results are **broadly stable under this specific leakage-aware grouping strategy**.

The rerun supports continued use of the baseline signal claim because the signal did not collapse when identical/near-similar grouping constraints were introduced through the committed manifest. It does not remove all leakage, similarity, provenance, or dataset-source concerns. It does not establish robust out-of-family generalization.
