# Review Outreach Templates v0.1

## Purpose

These are short outreach templates for sharing the current BBB-oriented manuscript package with trusted reviewers.

## Template A — Computational reviewer

I’m sharing a bounded BBB-oriented computational manuscript package for review. I’m sending the fuller packet because the most useful feedback would be on benchmark framing, artifact structure, reproducibility/evidence packaging, provenance clarity, and whether the claim boundary stays sufficiently tight.

## Template B — Biologically literate reviewer

I’m circulating an early BBB-oriented computational manuscript package and would value your read on whether the biological plausibility framing is responsible and whether the limitations are candid enough. I’m especially trying to avoid overclaiming, so feedback on scope discipline and overinterpretation risk would be very useful.

## Template C — General technical clarity reviewer

I’m sharing an early manuscript package for a bounded computational BBB-oriented study. The most useful feedback would be on readability, coherence, and whether the manuscript can be followed without much insider context. I’d also like to know whether the figures and document structure make the package easy to review.

## Notes on customization

- keep the reviewer ask narrow
- specify whether you are sending the minimum or extended packet
- ask for comments on claim boundary explicitly
- avoid overstating readiness or maturity
