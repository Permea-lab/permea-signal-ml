# Review Feedback Log v0.1

## Purpose

This log captures internal or trusted external review comments on the current BBB-oriented manuscript package.

## How to use this log

- use one row per feedback item
- separate the reviewer observation from the action decision
- distinguish wording fixes from structural issues
- keep claim-boundary issues highly visible

## Priority interpretation guide

- P0 — factual error, contradiction, or unsupported claim
- P1 — claim-boundary weakness or overclaim risk
- P2 — benchmark, provenance, dataset, or artifact clarity issue
- P3 — manuscript coherence, flow, or figure/table interpretability issue
- P4 — local wording, polish, or formatting issue

See `REVISION_PRIORITY_FRAMEWORK_V0_1.md` for the full triage rules.

Reviewer labels may use placeholders such as Reviewer A, Reviewer B, and Reviewer C. Do not add real reviewer identities to this log unless the repo is intended to track them.

## Action-status guide

- Accept — feedback will be adopted
- Reject — feedback will not be adopted
- Defer — feedback may be handled later
- Clarify — feedback needs discussion or narrowing
- Incorporated — change already made

## Initial review questions

- Is the claim boundary sufficiently clear?
- Does the paper overstate what the benchmark supports?
- Is the imported-versus-regenerated distinction easy to understand?
- Is the candidate-prioritization framing convincing?
- Are the limitations sufficiently explicit?
- Does the package read as a serious bounded computational study?
- Are the figures and tables sufficient for a first review round?
